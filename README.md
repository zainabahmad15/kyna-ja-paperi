# Kynä ja Paperi

## About Me

Hi, I'm Zainab Ahmad, a computer engineer and the founder of Kynä ja Paperi. I have a passion for creating unique, handcrafted stationery products that bring joy and personalization to everyday life.

## Project Overview

Kynä ja Paperi is a small business that specializes in designing and producing high-quality greeting cards, weddign stationary, and other paper goods. Our mission is to preserve the art of written communication and offer customers a carefully curated collection of items that cater to a variety of personal and professional needs.
